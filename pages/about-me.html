About Me

My Name is Tyler Clair and I am currently the Instructional Tools Administrator for Utah State University. My primary role is to administer our instance of the Instructure Canvas LMS, but I also administer numerous other systems that are used in conjunction with Canvas.

Currently I administer:

  • Instructure Canvas
  • Adobe Connect
  • WebEx
  • 3rd party LTI Tools
  • Panopto

I have a passion for tinkering, programming, 3D design, and 3D printing.
